⚠️ The Problem

The Problem We Solve

Every day, thousands of road accidents happen due to poor visibility, bad weather, and lack of driver awareness. Drivers often do not understand the risk level of the environment they are driving in, which leads to unsafe decisions.

🌧️

Bad Weather

Rain, fog, and storms drastically reduce visibility and increase accident risk.

😴

Lack of Awareness

Drivers are unaware of the current risk level of their surroundings while driving.

📱

No Single Tool

No tool gives weather, location risk, and safety advice all in one place.

⚠️

Accident-Prone Zones

Drivers pass through blackspots without any prior warning.

💡 Our Solution

Our Solution – RoadEye

RoadEye is an intelligent system that monitors real-time environmental conditions and predicts accident risk. It provides drivers with instant alerts and safety recommendations to help them make better decisions on the road.

  • No need to open a separate weather app — weather, risk and advisory all in one place.
  • Understand whether conditions are safe, moderate, or dangerous instantly.
  • Simple safety tips tailored to the current environment.
  • View risk history to understand patterns in your regular routes.
✅
