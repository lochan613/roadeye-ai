Essential Safety Tips
Following Distance Maintain 3 seconds gap from the vehicle ahead
Wet Roads Brake gently and early — avoid sudden stops
Fog & Rain Reduce speed 30%, use headlights and fog lights
Night Driving Use high beams on empty roads, switch to low in traffic
Driver Fatigue Take a break every 2 hours — fatigue causes 20% of accidents
Dust Storms Stop safely off road, keep hazard lights on till storm passes
